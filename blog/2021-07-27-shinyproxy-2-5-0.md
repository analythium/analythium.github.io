The ShinyProxy 1-Click App in the DigitalOcean Marketplace was recently updated to ShinyProxy 2.5.0.

[ShinyProxy](https://www.shinyproxy.io/) is an open source platform to deploy R Shiny applications at scale. The 1-click option lets you deploy ShinyProxy on DigitalOcean droplets in fully-tested app environments. The new version of the 1-click App now has ShinyProxy version 2.5.0.
